## March 9, 2026 — New Feature

### Invite & Earn

Invite friends to join AnyFast and earn rewards when they top up their account. The more friends you invite, the more you earn.

![Invite rewards panel](/images/changelog/invite/invite.png)

**How it works:**

1. Go to the [Top-up page](https://www.anyfast.ai/console/topup) and scroll to the **Invite Rewards** section at the bottom
2. Copy your unique invite link and share it with friends
3. When a friend signs up through your link and tops up, you receive a reward
4. Transfer your reward credits to your account balance using the transfer feature

**Reward rules:**

- You earn a reward when your invited friend makes their first top-up
- Rewards are held as pending credits until transferred to your main balance
