Upmore uses a prepaid credit system. You add credits to your account and they are deducted as you make API calls — per token for chat models, per generation for image and video models.

## Top up your account

Visit the [Top Up page](https://api.upmore.net/console/topup) in the Console to add credits to your account.

>

</Card>

## Supported payment methods

- **Credit / Debit card** — Visa, Mastercard, American Express
- **Alipay**
- **WeChat Pay**

## How billing works

- Credits are deducted in real time as you make API calls
- Chat models are billed **per token** (input + output)
- Image and video generation models are billed **per generation**
- Unused credits do not expire

## Checking your balance

You can view your current credit balance and usage history in the [Console](https://api.upmore.net/console/topup).
